About Us

The East African Bridge
to DRC Gold

EastBridge Gold Dealers is a professional gold trading and export company headquartered in Kampala, Uganda. We operate under full authorisation from the Ministry of Energy and Minerals of Uganda — legally licensed to procure, trade, and export gold and precious minerals.

We source premium natural gold directly from the Democratic Republic of Congo, one of the world's most mineral-abundant territories. Our supply chain is established, verified, and traceable — giving international buyers the confidence and compliance they need.

We serve refineries, bullion dealers, importers, and institutional buyers across Europe, the Middle East, Asia, and the Americas. Our commitment: quality gold, honest pricing, and complete paperwork on every deal.
